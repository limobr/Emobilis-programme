Kibandaski
Nestled in the heart of Kabianga, Kericho, Kibanda Ski is more than just a restaurant; it's a culinary journey that celebrates the rich flavors of Kenyan cuisine. Our commitment to quality, taste, and exceptional service has made us a beloved local eatery. We pride ourselves on using fresh, locally sourced ingredients to create dishes that tantalize your taste buds.

Whether you're in the mood for a traditional Kenyan meal or international favorites, our skilled chefs have got you covered.
